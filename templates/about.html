Who We Are

We are NoBs, a data science and machine learning company headquartered in Kerala, India—a region known for its highly literate and intelligent workforce, despite being underutilized due to political and economic challenges. At NoBs, we focus on one principle: creating real value for our clients. If there’s no value, you pay nothing—our "No Value, Full Refund" guarantee.

Vision

To democratize AI by making it affordable, practical, and impactful for businesses of all sizes.

Mission

To deliver measurable value through cost-effective AI solutions while leveraging Kerala’s talent and open-source technologies.
